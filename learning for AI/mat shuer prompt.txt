## PROCESS INSTRUCTIONS

1. First, develop three distinct, realistic personas representing different segments of the target market.
2. Generate three initial ad concepts tailored to the product/service and target audience.
3. Have each persona evaluate each ad concept, providing detailed feedback on what works, what doesn't, and suggestions for improvement.
4. Create refined versions of the ads based on persona feedback.
5. Repeat the feedback and refinement process at least three times or until all personas strongly approve of at least one ad concept.
6. Select the winning ad concept that has the strongest consensus among personas and provide implementation guidance.
